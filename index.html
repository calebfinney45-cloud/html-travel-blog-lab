Adventures in Fire Island

My recent trip to Fire Island was an unforgettable escape into one of the most unique National Parks on the East Coast. From hiking through the rare holly trees of the Sunken Forest to climbing the historic Fire Island Lighthouse, the scenery was breath-taking. The island's car-free boardwalks and pristine white sand dunes make it the perfect place for anyone looking to reconnect with nature.
